♉ Taurus Love Tarot — July 2026

This July, taurus, your love life is parked in a comfortable rut you refuse to leave. Stubbornness and a quiet pessimism have walled off new warmth, and the month asks one thing of you: rest, soften, and stop guarding the gate so tightly before love can move.

Your three cards for July 2026

  • Where your heart stands now
    Knight of PentaclesKnReversed

    Your heart has settled into a groove so familiar it stopped feeling like comfort and started feeling like a cage. The Knight of Pentacles reversed catches the taurus you become when security curdles into inertia: too stubborn to go out, too tired to try, raising your standards just to dodge the effort of meeting someone. You call it being selective. Deep down you know it's a rut.

  • What love is moving toward
    The SunXIXReversed

    Joy is trying to reach you, and you keep dimming it yourself. The Sun reversed shows love moving toward you slowly, snagged on the perfect checklist a taurus clings to and the private belief that it won't work out anyway. Warmth is already near, smaller and plainer than your standards demand. Loosen the grip on flawless, and the simple, real thing waiting in your blind spot finally gets through.

  • The cards’ guidance this month
    Four of SwordsIV

    Stop forcing love this month and rest instead. The Four of Swords lands as permission the security-loving taurus rarely grants yourself: retreat, breathe, let the tiredness drain out before you invite anyone in. You don't need a new person to feel whole right now; you need quiet. Spend July tending your own ground. The loyalty you pour so freely into others, turn it inward first, and you'll meet the next one from fullness instead of fatigue.

How to read this month’s spread

Read the three together. The first names where your heart genuinely stands right now, under the noise. The second shows the direction love is drifting if you keep doing what you are doing. The third is the steadying word to carry, a pointer rather than an order. Nothing here is fixed; it is the shape of the month, clear enough to move through with your eyes open.

A monthly love reading is a mirror for reflection, not a forecast to hand your heart to. The choices stay yours.
