Veila Tarot Summary / บทสรุป Veila

Veila is a quiet, bilingual tarot practice for life's turning points. It begins with Love — over 100 reflective readings of the heart in English and Thai — with Career readings arriving next and Health to follow. Every reading uses the 78 Rider–Waite–Smith archetypes for grounded self-awareness, not prediction.
