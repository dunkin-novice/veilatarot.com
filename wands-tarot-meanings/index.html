Fire · Will · Vocation

The Suit of Wands

Wands are fire — the energy of will, ambition, vocation, and the creative spark. They speak to what you are driving toward and what is driving you.

A Wand in a spread is rarely passive. The question is usually about initiative, momentum, or the heat of an undertaking. Wands ask you to either light something or admit the match is already in your hand.
