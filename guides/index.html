Reading well

Tarot Guides

Most flat readings come from the setup, not the cards — a question phrased as a coin flip, a spread that doesn't match what is being asked. These three guides cover the craft that happens before the first card is turned.

The guides

  1. I
    How to Ask a Tarot Question
    The shape of the question shapes the answer — softening closed prediction questions into ones the cards can meet.
  2. II
    Tarot Spreads for Relationships
    Four three-card spreads — Connection, Emotional Arc, Clarity, Reconnection — and which question each one is built for.
  3. III
    Tarot for Reflection
    The practice itself — using the cards to notice emotional patterns and slow a hard question down.

Put a guide to work

Once the question is phrased, the readings are a step away. The love questions index collects the questions people bring most often, each with its own spread; love readings organises them by scenario; and the about page explains how every reading on Veila is made.
