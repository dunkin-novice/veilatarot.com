For matters of the heart

Tarot for Love & Relationships

Tarot is a poor crystal ball and an unusually good mirror. For love questions, that distinction matters — the cards rarely tell you what someone else will do, but they read with surprising clarity what you are bringing to the room.

When you sit with a love question, hold it specifically. Not 'will I find love?' but 'what am I carrying into this relationship?' or 'what is between us that needs naming?' The cards below come up often in love readings — not because they predict romance, but because each names a recognizable emotional posture.

Common cards in love readings

  1. VI
    The Lovers
    love, choices, harmony, heart's crossroads, commitment
  2. III
    The Empress
    abundance, attraction, nature, birth, physical pleasure
  3. II
    The High Priestess
    intuition, mystery, subconscious, inner wisdom, stillness
  4. I
    Ace of Cups
    new love, overflowing emotion, healing, intuition, emotional opportunity
  5. II
    Two of Cups
    mutual attraction, partnership, connection, harmony, shared feelings
  6. III
    Three of Cups
    celebration, friendship, joy, community, reunion
  7. VI
    Six of Cups
    nostalgia, old friends, innocence, homecoming, simple joys
  8. X
    Ten of Cups
    ultimate happiness, happy family, harmony, fulfillment, peace
  9. Kn
    Knight of Cups
    romance, following the heart, emotional offer, imagination, charm
  10. Qn
    Queen of Cups
    empathy, intuition, emotional healing, compassion, emotional intelligence
  11. IV
    Four of Wands
    celebration, stability, homecoming, joy, harmony
