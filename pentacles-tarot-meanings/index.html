The Suit of Pentacles

Pentacles are earth — the material plane. Body, home, craft, money, the slow accumulation of skill and resource.

Pentacles often look unglamorous on the surface and turn out to be the most practically useful cards in the deck. They answer questions about livelihood, health, and the kind of patient work that compounds over years.
