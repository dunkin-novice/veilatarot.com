What part of myself am I hiding from potential partners?

To answer “What part of myself am I hiding from potential partners?”, Veila draws three cards, one for the Armor You Wear, one for what It Protects, and one for what Wants to Be Seen. Because each card holds a written interpretation for every position of this spread, no two readings of this question come out the same.

This is one of those questions that sits underneath the whole day. Drawing three cards is a way of letting it speak.

How this reading works

This page connects to Veila's Quick Love Reading. When you draw your three cards, each one opens an interpretation written specifically for “What part of myself am I hiding from potential partners?”, not a generic card meaning. Every card in the deck has its own answer for every position of this spread.

The three positions of this spread

  1. The Armor You WearYour opening card speaks to the Armor You Wear, the part of the answer that is already in motion.
  2. What It ProtectsThe middle card carries what It Protects, the quieter truth sitting under the first.
  3. What Wants to Be SeenCard three settles on what Wants to Be Seen, the step the reading leaves in your hands.

What this reading covers

In practice, that means the reading covers where things genuinely stand, what has been sitting underneath, and what you can do next. It will not decide for you, and it is not meant to.

Treat what you draw as reflection, not instruction. The final word on your own heart is still yours.
