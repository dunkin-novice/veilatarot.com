Major Arcana · II

The High Priestess

The Oracle

The High Priestess — Upright

intuition, mystery, subconscious, inner wisdom, stillness

The answers you want aren't out there in the noise; they're in the quiet space inside you. Trust that gut feeling—it knows more than your logic does right now.

The High Priestess — Reversed

Blocked Intuition, Leaked Secrets, Emotional Confusion, Superficiality, Isolation

Your inner radio signal is static right now; you are confusing fear with intuition. You might be over-analyzing everything to drown out a feeling you don't want to acknowledge.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How The High Priestess reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

There is a deep stillness and a sense of hidden knowledge surrounding you now. You are being called to wait, listen, and look beyond the surface of things rather than forcing an external outcome.

Reversed

The inner voice is being drowned out by external noise. You are currently in a state of psychic clutter, ignoring the red flags and gut feelings that are trying to guide you through the fog.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The challenge is your tendency to stay in the shadows or keep secrets to the point of isolation. Being too 'mysterious' or relying solely on intuition while ignoring hard facts is creating a barrier to progress.

Reversed

Your challenge is an active avoidance of the truth. You are looking for logical answers to a spiritual problem, or perhaps refusing to sit in silence because you’re afraid of what you’ll hear in the stillness.

III. The Past

Upright

You’ve recently gone through a phase of internal reflection or study, perhaps pulling back from the world to understand yourself better. This period of withdrawal has sharpened your inner vision.

Reversed

A recent period of 'keeping secrets' or perhaps a lack of transparency has led to the current confusion. You may have ignored your intuition in a past decision and are now dealing with the internal dissonance.

IV. The Future

Upright

A revelation or a secret will soon be uncovered, but only if you remain observant. The next phase requires you to trust the timing of the universe and keep your cards close to your chest.

Reversed

Prepare for a revelation that disrupts your peace. A secret may come to light, or a situation you've been 'sensing' will finally manifest in a way that can no longer be ignored.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

You are consciously seeking spiritual depth or trying to decode a mystery. Your mind is focused on finding the 'hidden' meaning in your current situation, prioritizing wisdom over activity.

Reversed

Consciously, you are over-analyzing the situation, trying to find secrets or 'hidden meanings' where there are none, while ignoring the obvious reality right in front of your eyes.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

Your subconscious is incredibly active, delivering messages through dreams and gut feelings that you haven't quite articulated yet. There is a wellspring of feminine power and psychic energy fueling you.

Reversed

Hidden underneath is a fear of your own intuitive power or a deep mistrust of your sensitive side. You may be suppressing a secret or an uncomfortable truth that is starting to leak out through your dreams.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are acting as the silent observer, withholding judgment and staying private. You are currently more comfortable with questions than with answers, trusting your internal compass above all else.

Reversed

You feel disconnected from your center, acting on impulse or surface-level information rather than deep wisdom. You are behaving as if you are someone startled by its own shadow—unnecessarily jumpy and out of alignment.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The environment is shrouded in mystery or is not yet ready to reveal its full truth. Others may be keeping information from you, or the setting itself demands a high degree of discretion and sensitivity.

Reversed

The environment is filled with gossip, misinformation, or shallow distractions. There is a lack of depth in your current social interactions, making it hard to find the clarity you seek from others.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You hope for a profound spiritual breakthrough, but you fear that the truth you find will be too heavy to carry or that you are simply dreaming and misreading the signs.

Reversed

You hope for clarity to magically appear, yet you fear that your intuition is 'broken' or that looking too deeply into the situation will reveal a truth you aren't ready to handle.

X. Outcome

Upright

If you continue to listen to your inner voice, the situation will settle into a state of deep clarity and grace. The outcome is not a loud victory, but a quiet, unshakable knowing that you are on the right path.

Reversed

The path leads toward a state of spiritual exhaustion until you stop fighting the silence. If you continue to ignore your inner guidance, the truth will eventually force its way out in a much more disruptive manner.
