Wheel of Fortune

The Spinner

Wheel of Fortune — Upright

destiny, change, life cycles, timing, coincidence

The wheel is spinning fast! Unexpected changes are shifting your reality right now. Don't resist the motion—destiny is working hard to throw a lucky opportunity your way.

Wheel of Fortune — Reversed

Bad luck, Resistance to change, Repetitive cycles, Delays, Misalignment

Right now, it feels like the universe is hitting the pause button. Old problems are circling back like a bad sequel, forcing you to deal with them before the wheel spins forward again.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How Wheel of Fortune reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

A major cycle is turning, bringing a sense of momentum and shifting tides that feel larger than your individual will.

Reversed

Everything feels stuck or out of sync, as if the natural flow of your life has hit a frustrating snag.

II. The Challenge

Upright

ความท้าทายคือการที่คุณควบคุมอะไรไม่ได้เลย คุณกำลังพยายามทรงตัวในขณะที่พื้นข้างล่างกำลังขยับเขยื้อนอยู่ตลอดเวลา

Reversed

The friction comes from your intense resistance to an inevitable change; you are trying to stop a wheel that must turn.

III. The Past

Upright

A recent stroke of luck or a closed chapter has cleared the way for this new, unpredictable energy to enter.

Reversed

A sudden, unwelcome shift or a missed opportunity recently knocked you off balance, and you're still feeling dizzy.

IV. The Future

Upright

The pace is about to accelerate; expect a 'right place, right time' encounter that changes your trajectory.

Reversed

Expect a period of forced stillness; the universe is hitting the pause button so you can re-evaluate your direction.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

You are focused on catching a 'big break' or trying to predict the next trend to stay ahead of the curve.

Reversed

You are mentally preoccupied with controlling every small detail to prevent a perceived catastrophe or setback.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

A subconscious belief in karma or a deep readiness for change is fueling this sudden shift in your external reality.

Reversed

At the root, there is a lingering belief that you are merely a victim of bad luck, making it hard to reclaim your power.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are trying to remain the calm center of the storm, realizing that you cannot stop the wheel from spinning.

Reversed

You feel caught in a loop, exhausted by your own repetitive patterns and looking for a way out.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The situation is highly volatile and influenced by external factors—markets, timing, or coincidences—that you didn't create.

Reversed

External circumstances feel chaotic and unstable, with outside forces seemingly conspiring to delay your plans.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You hope for a sudden windfall or a turnaround, but you dread the 'down' cycle that inevitably follows every peak.

Reversed

You hope for a lucky break to solve it all, yet you fear that you've somehow lost your 'magic touch' forever.

X. Outcome

Upright

If you adapt to the rhythm of the change rather than resisting it, the wheel will land you in a significantly improved position.

Reversed

The wheel will eventually regain its momentum, but only after you accept the lessons hidden in this current stagnation.
