Major Arcana · XI

Justice

The Balancer

Justice — Upright

fairness, truth, karma, balance, logical decision-making

Karma is serving you exactly what you ordered. You are receiving what you put out, so if you need to make a decision today, keep it strictly fair and absolutely bias-free.

Justice — Reversed

Injustice, Avoiding accountability, Bias, Dishonesty, Imbalance

The scales are tipped. You might feel the world is treating you unfairly right now, but ask yourself honestly: are you refusing to take responsibility for your own part in this mess?

In the Celtic Cross spread

How Justice reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

The scales are active; you are currently dealing with a situation where truth, fairness, and accountability are the primary themes.

Reversed

A situation in your life is currently marked by imbalance, unfairness, or a refusal to face the consequences.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The demand for objectivity is high, making it difficult to act when your emotions are pulling you toward a biased or defensive stance.

Reversed

The core challenge is a lack of accountability—either you are avoiding the truth or someone is playing unfair with you.

III. The Past

Upright

A decision made or an agreement signed in the recent past is now the legal or moral foundation for your current reality.

Reversed

A recent decision made in haste or based on skewed information is what led to this current state of disharmony.

IV. The Future

Upright

A clear verdict or a restoration of balance is coming; things will soon be called by their true names.

Reversed

Unresolved legal or ethical issues are likely to resurface soon, demanding a more honest and objective approach.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

You are consciously striving to make the 'right' choice, focusing on ethics, contracts, or a long-overdue resolution.

Reversed

You are consciously fixated on how you've been wronged, perhaps missing your own role in the current complexity.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

Underneath it all, there is a deep need for validation or a quiet anxiety about whether past actions are finally catching up to you.

Reversed

Deep down, there is a hidden bias or a moral compromise you’ve made that is now weighing heavy on your spirit.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are assuming the role of the impartial judge, trying to weigh all sides of the story before making your move.

Reversed

You are feeling defensive and perhaps a bit victimized, ready to lash out because the scales don't seem to tip in your favor.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

You are surrounded by systems of rules, legalities, or people who are holding you strictly accountable for your word.

Reversed

You are navigating an environment where the rules are unclear or where someone in power is acting with favoritism.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You hope for an outcome that is fair to you, but you fear that 'fair' might not mean getting exactly what you want.

Reversed

You hope for a swift vindication, but you fear that the full truth might actually be inconvenient for your current position.

X. Outcome

Upright

The situation will resolve with total clarity; the dust will settle in a way that aligns perfectly with the energy put into it.

Reversed

The situation is heading toward a messy resolution unless a rigorous effort toward radical honesty is made now.
