Major Arcana · XIX

The Sun

The Radiant Child

The Sun — Upright

happiness, success, vitality, revelation, clarity

It's your time to shine! No need to worry about hidden traps; everything is bright and clear. Step out into the sunlight and enjoy this wave of happiness.

The Sun — Reversed

Diminished joy, Delayed success, Pessimism, Ego, Temporary burnout

The sun is still there, you're just under a temporary cloud. You might be feeling bratty or overlooking the good stuff because it's not "perfect." Stop pouting.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How The Sun reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

You are standing in the ultimate sunny spot. Everything is visible, radiant, and filled with a sense of uncomplicated joy and vital energy.

Reversed

The light is there, but it feels like you're looking at it through a heavy curtain. There is joy available to you, but it’s currently muted or delayed by a sense of 'not quite yet.'

II. The Challenge

Upright

The glare might be so bright that it’s blinding you to the nuances or making you feel exposed. Success here brings the pressure to stay 'on' and keep performing at your peak.

Reversed

The challenge is a struggle with pessimism or burnt-out expectations. You might be over-complicating something that is actually simple, or perhaps you're being too hard on your inner child for not feeling 'sunnier.'

III. The Past

Upright

A breakthrough or a moment of total clarity recently paved the way for the abundance you are experiencing now.

Reversed

A recent moment of glory may have felt bittersweet, or perhaps a project you were excited about didn't get the 'standing ovation' you expected, leading to this current dip in confidence.

IV. The Future

Upright

The path ahead is clear and inviting. Expect a period of high visibility where your efforts are recognized and rewarded with warmth.

Reversed

There is a need to recalibrate your definition of success. The near future isn't about grand explosions of light, but about finding a steady, sustainable warmth that doesn't burn you out.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

Your focus is entirely on success, expansion, and celebrating your achievements. You are consciously leaning into optimism and looking for the win.

Reversed

You are consciously trying to 'fake it until you make it.' You’re focusing on looking successful or happy to the outside world, even if the internal battery is at 5%.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

A core sense of inner worth and childlike wonder is fueling your drive. Deep down, you are healing old wounds by finally allowing yourself to be seen and celebrated.

Reversed

Beneath the surface lies a core belief that high points never last. This subconscious cynicism is acting as a dimmer switch, preventing you from fully basking in your current wins.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are radiating confidence and authenticity. You’re showing up as your most vibrant self, ready to play and lead with an open heart.

Reversed

You’re feeling a bit as if you are someone hiding under the bed on a summer day. You know it’s nice outside, but you don't feel ready to come out and play just yet.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The atmosphere around you is supportive and celebratory. You are surrounded by people who reflect your light and encourage your growth.

Reversed

The people around you might be overly loud or demanding of your energy. You may feel pressured to be the 'life of the party' when all you want is a little shade and some quiet.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You hope for this state of bliss to be permanent, yet you fear the inevitable setting of the sun or that this joy is 'too good to be true.'

Reversed

You hope for a breakthrough that restores your vitality, but you fear that you’ve lost your spark for good or that your best days are behind you.

X. Outcome

Upright

If you keep basking in this truth, you will reach a state of absolute fulfillment where your external success perfectly matches your internal happiness.

Reversed

The outcome is a quiet realization that happiness doesn't have to be performative. You will find your glow again, but it will be softer, more internal, and far more resilient than before.
