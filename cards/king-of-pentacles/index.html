King of Pentacles

The Steward of Wealth

King of Pentacles — Upright

ultimate wealth, business success, stability, reliable leadership, abundance

You sit at the top. It is time to enjoy your success and use your influence and resources to expand your empire and take care of the people who depend on you.

King of Pentacles — Reversed

Greed, Corruption, Materialism, Business failure, Tyranny

You are gripping your money (or your power) too tight. Obsessing over status and wealth is making you cold and lonely. Remember, you can own things, but don't let them own you.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How King of Pentacles reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

You are currently standing on solid ground, acting with the authority and confidence of a master. The situation is focused on high-level management, wealth, and long-term stability.

Reversed

There is a misuse of power or a focus on material gains that has become cold and transactional. The heart has been traded for a calculator.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The challenge is a potential for rigidity or becoming too 'bottom-line' focused. You might be resisting a necessary change because it feels risky, or your obsession with status is making you emotionally distant.

Reversed

The challenge is a rigid, controlling energy—either your own or someone else's—that refuses to see the human element in a business or financial situation.

III. The Past

Upright

You have recently completed a major project or investment that solidified your reputation. Your past successes are exactly what gave you the 'throne' you sit on today.

Reversed

A period of material success that came at a high personal cost, or perhaps a leadership role that left you feeling isolated and cynical.

IV. The Future

Upright

The near future points to a position of even greater influence or the formalization of a business success. Expect to be seen as the ultimate authority in your field.

Reversed

A situation is approaching where your integrity regarding money or authority will be tested. Be wary of taking shortcuts for the sake of profit.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

You are consciously thinking about your legacy and the expansion of your influence. Your goals are big, practical, and ambitious, focused on building something that lasts.

Reversed

You are focusing on 'empire building' and long-term security, but perhaps in a way that is overly defensive or greedy, shutting out collaboration.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

Deep down, you are driven by a need for total control over your physical world. This stems from a core belief that mastery over one's resources is the only true way to protect oneself and others.

Reversed

Deep down, there is an intense fear of loss or poverty that drives you to over-control your resources and keep a tight grip on everything you own.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are showing up with extreme confidence and business savvy. You feel like the owner of the situation, capable of turning any resource into gold through sheer competence.

Reversed

You may be acting with a 'my way or the highway' attitude, prioritizing efficiency and bottom lines over the well-being of yourself and those around you.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The environment is one of high stakes and professional competition. You are surrounded by established structures or people who value results above all else.

Reversed

The environment is competitive, corrupt, or overly focused on hierarchy, making it difficult to trust the intentions of those in power.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You hope to reach the absolute peak of material success, but you fear the isolation that often comes with being the one at the top who has to make the hard calls.

Reversed

You hope for ultimate financial freedom, but you fear that you’ve become the very thing you once despised: a cold, unyielding gatekeeper.

X. Outcome

Upright

The situation is leading toward total stability and the achievement of a major material goal. You will likely secure a lasting victory that provides safety for years to come.

Reversed

If this path continues, you may achieve your financial goals but find yourself sitting on a lonely throne. True wealth requires a more generous and ethical spirit.
