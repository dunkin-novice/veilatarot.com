Minor Arcana · Cups

Page of Cups

The Messenger of Love

Page of Cups — Upright

emotional message, creative spark, intuition, inner child, romantic proposal

Surprise! A sweet message or a quirky creative idea is popping up out of nowhere (like a fish in a cup!). Don't overthink it—just follow that flutter of excitement.

Page of Cups — Reversed

Emotional immaturity, Bad news, Unrequited love, Delusion, Creative block

You are feeling extra sensitive and fragile right now. Watch out for mood swings or getting upset over rumors that might not be true. Don't let your inner child throw a tantrum.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How Page of Cups reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

A tender, new emotional seed is sprouting, marked by a sense of wonder and a slightly shy curiosity.

Reversed

You are experiencing a creative block or an emotional fog. The messages you're receiving—or sending—feel garbled, immature, or disconnected from your true intuition.

II. The Challenge

Upright

A tendency toward hypersensitivity or getting so lost in the 'dream' of a thing that you ignore the practical reality.

Reversed

The challenge is your tendency to get distracted by shiny emotional lures or 'escapism.' You are chasing laser pointers instead of looking at the source of your feelings.

III. The Past

Upright

An unexpected piece of news or a small, sweet gesture that reawakened your emotional world recently.

Reversed

A recent disappointment in a creative project or a slight emotional snub has left you feeling deflated and less willing to share your 'art' with the world.

IV. The Future

Upright

An invitation to explore a new creative hobby or a soft, emerging connection with someone like-minded.

Reversed

The near future holds a moment where a fantasy will be punctured by reality. It’s a necessary wake-up call to help you ground your sensitivity into something tangible.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

You are consciously trying to be more open-hearted and receptive to intuitive messages or creative sparks.

Reversed

Mentally, you are seeking inspiration or a sign to move forward, but your ego is currently too fragile to handle constructive feedback or the reality of the work involved.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

A childlike part of your spirit is resurfacing, needing permission to play and express feelings without judgment.

Reversed

Deep down, there is a wounded inner self that is afraid of being laughed at. This insecurity is causing you to act out or retreat into a world of pure fantasy.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are showing up with a 'beginner's mind,' sensitive and ready to learn the language of your heart.

Reversed

You are appearing as someone who is emotionally unreliable or perhaps overly sensitive to criticism right now, struggling to find a solid internal footing.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

A social setting that rewards vulnerability, though it may feel a bit unpredictable or whimsical.

Reversed

The environment is full of mixed signals and people who are not being entirely honest with their feelings, creating an atmosphere of subtle drama and misunderstanding.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

Hoping for a magical, poetic connection while fearing you'll look foolish or naive for being so open.

Reversed

You hope for a magical breakthrough that validates your intuition, yet you fear that you're just making it all up and have no 'real' talent or depth.

X. Outcome

Upright

If you follow this curiosity, you will find a new way to express your innermost feelings with clarity and art.

Reversed

If you don't ground yourself, the situation will likely dissipate into a series of missed connections and half-finished ideas that leave you feeling emotionally spent.
