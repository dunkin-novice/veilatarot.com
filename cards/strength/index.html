Major Arcana · VIII

Strength

The Inner Lion

Strength — Upright

courage, patience, compassion, self-control, inner power

Don't roar; just breathe. Handle this situation with gentle confidence. You can tame any problem (or person) today with kindness, not anger.

Strength — Reversed

Self-doubt, Weakness, Insecurity, Explosive Temper, Lack of Discipline

You're trying to project strength but your confidence feels thin right now. Self-doubt is heavy, making you feel weak or causing you to lash out defensively at others.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How Strength reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

You are currently navigating a sensitive situation with immense grace and patience. The central focus is on mastering your internal reactions rather than trying to dominate external events.

Reversed

You are currently grappling with self-doubt and emotional fatigue. The 'inner beast' isn't being tamed with love right now; instead, it feels like your insecurities are leading the way.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The challenge lies in the difficulty of remaining gentle when you want to roar. You may be struggling with self-suppression or finding it exhausting to 'be the bigger person' in a recurring conflict.

Reversed

The challenge is your own internal critic and a lack of self-discipline. You are letting your rawest emotions dictate your actions, making it hard to maintain the quiet courage needed to move forward.

III. The Past

Upright

You have recently endured a test of character that required significant restraint. A period of high stress has passed, leaving you with a newfound sense of quiet confidence.

Reversed

A recent situation has drained your reserves of patience and inner power. You may have experienced a moment where you felt 'weak' or unable to hold your ground, and that memory is still stinging.

IV. The Future

Upright

The near future will require you to use soft power to tame a brewing conflict. You are approaching a moment where your persuasive nature will be much more effective than any ultimatum.

Reversed

Expect a period where your patience will be tested further, requiring you to find a softer, non-combative way to handle stress. The focus will shift to slow, internal healing.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

Consciously, you are trying to stay composed and compassionate. You are aiming for a solution that avoids brute force, prioritizing emotional intelligence and long-term stability.

Reversed

On a conscious level, you are trying to 'act' strong or force yourself to be resilient, but this performance is only making you feel more hollow. You are preoccupied with appearances of strength.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

At the root, there is a powerful reserve of courage and animal instinct that is being carefully channeled. This situation is fueled by a deep-seated resilience you didn’t know you had.

Reversed

At the root, there is an old wound regarding your worth or a basic instinct of fear that hasn't been addressed. Your subconscious is stuck in a 'defend and withdraw' defensive mode.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are showing up as a person of high integrity and inner quietude. You feel capable of handling the 'lion' in the room without fear, relying on your internal stability.

Reversed

You feel physically or emotionally depleted, as if you are still learning facing a world that feels too big and loud. Your self-esteem is currently at a low ebb, making you feel vulnerable.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The surrounding conditions are volatile, and people may be looking to you to provide a calming influence. You are in a space where raw emotions are high, requiring your steadying hand.

Reversed

The environment feels demanding or perhaps insensitive to your current fragility. You might feel surrounded by 'lions' that you don't have the energy to soothe or manage right now.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You hope to achieve a peaceful resolution where everyone feels respected, but you fear that your patience might be mistaken for weakness by those who value force.

Reversed

You hope to regain your inner fire and grace, but you are terrified that you’ve lost your 'defenses' and will never be able to stand up for yourself again.

X. Outcome

Upright

If you maintain your composure, you will achieve a lasting victory of the spirit. The path leads toward mastery over the situation, where conflict is resolved through understanding rather than combat.

Reversed

If you continue to judge yourself for being 'weak,' you'll remain stuck in a cycle of insecurity. The outcome points toward a need to embrace your vulnerability as a form of truth, not a flaw.
