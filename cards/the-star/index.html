Major Arcana · XVII

The Star

The Light

The Star — Upright

hope, healing, inspiration, serenity, renewed faith

This is your moment to breathe. The universe is signaling that you are safe and on the right path. Open your heart—hope and inspiration are flowing back in.

The Star — Reversed

Despair, Lack of faith, Broken dreams, Uninspired, Discouragement

You are staring at the mud and forgetting the sky exists. You feel disconnected, uninspired, and maybe a bit cynical. The universe hasn't left you; you just stopped looking up.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How The Star reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

You are currently entering a phase of profound calm and inspiration. After a period of chaos, the dust is settling, and you are beginning to feel a renewed sense of hope and spiritual alignment.

Reversed

The current atmosphere is one of dimmed inspiration; you are in a phase where hope feels distant or your sense of purpose is temporarily clouded.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The challenge here is 'passive optimism.' You may be so comforted by the glimmer of hope that you forget to take the practical steps needed to ground that vision into reality. Inspiration without action can become a beautiful distraction.

Reversed

The challenge is a creeping cynicism or 'spiritual burnout'; it's hard to stay open to healing when you feel like the universe has stopped listening.

III. The Past

Upright

You have recently navigated a significant crisis or upheaval. The Star appears now because you have successfully shed the old skin and are ready to be replenished.

Reversed

A period of high idealism or a dream that didn't manifest as expected has recently left you feeling deflated and skeptical.

IV. The Future

Upright

Expect a gentle flow of inspiration and unexpected synchronicities. The path ahead is clear and guided, leading you toward a period of creative and emotional restoration.

Reversed

The near future requires you to ground your dreams in reality; you'll need to focus on small, tangible steps rather than waiting for a grand sign.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

Consciously, you are looking at the 'big picture' and focusing on healing. You are dreaming big and allowing yourself to believe that things will finally work out for the best.

Reversed

Consciously, you are trying to find logical reasons for your lack of progress, focusing on practical flaws rather than emotional replenishment.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

At your core, there is an unshakeable faith that the universe is supporting you. This deep-seated serenity is acting as a natural reservoir of strength, helping you stay soft even when things are hard.

Reversed

At your root, there is a deep reservoir of unaddressed disappointment or a feeling of being 'lost in the dark' that you haven't dared to name.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You are showing up with vulnerability and grace. You are being asked to be your most authentic self, pouring your energy out freely without fear of running dry.

Reversed

You are appearing as someone who has lost their 'spark,' perhaps feeling uncreative or disconnected from the intuitive flow that usually guides you.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

Your surroundings are becoming more peaceful and supportive. You may find yourself in spaces (or with people) that encourage your healing and mirror your internal sense of serenity.

Reversed

The external setting is uninspiring or overly critical, offering very little emotional nourishment or space for your vulnerability.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You desperately want to believe in this bright future, but there is a lingering fear that it’s just an illusion—that the star will fade and leave you in the dark again.

Reversed

You hope to find your way back to a place of faith and peace, but you fear that you’ve permanently lost your connection to your higher path.

X. Outcome

Upright

If you maintain this openness, the situation will settle into a long-term state of balance and fulfillment. You are moving toward a version of your life that feels both inspired and deeply authentic.

Reversed

This pattern leads toward a necessary period of 'internal watering'; you will eventually realize that hope is a discipline, not just a feeling you wait for.
