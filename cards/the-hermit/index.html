Major Arcana · IX

The Hermit

The Seeker

The Hermit — Upright

soul-searching, solitude, inner wisdom, withdrawal, guiding light

The world is too loud right now. Turn off your phone, retreat to your cave, and listen to the silence. Your inner voice has the answer you need.

The Hermit — Reversed

Isolation, Loneliness, Withdrawal, Rejecting help, Lost in the dark

It is dark in your cave, isn't it? You are feeling lonely even in a crowd, or you are hiding from reality. Your lantern has gone out, and it is time to let someone else shine a light for you.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How The Hermit reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

You are currently in a sacred period of withdrawal, pulling your energy inward to find a truth that the outside world cannot give you.

Reversed

You are currently drifting in a fog of isolation, feeling disconnected from both your inner guidance and the world around you.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The silence is becoming a wall; your need for solitude might be morphing into counterproductive isolation or a refusal to hear outside perspectives.

Reversed

The challenge is a stubborn refusal to look inward, or perhaps getting so lost in your own thoughts that you've lost the path back to reality.

III. The Past

Upright

A recent phase of intense study or a deliberate choice to step out of the spotlight has brought you to this threshold.

Reversed

A recent period of forced loneliness or a social withdrawal that went on a little too long has left you feeling socially rusty.

IV. The Future

Upright

A significant 'aha' moment is approaching, where the pieces of your internal puzzle finally click into a coherent picture.

Reversed

The near future calls for an end to your self-imposed exile; the light is flickering, prompting you to step out of the dark.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

You are consciously seeking a mentor, a deeper meaning, or a logical 'why' behind your current life path.

Reversed

Consciously, you are trying to project an image of having it all figured out, even while you feel internally directionless.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

Deep down, there is a soul-level exhaustion and a profound need to reconnect with your own rhythm away from societal expectations.

Reversed

Underneath, there is a deep-seated fear of what you might discover if you truly sat in silence with your own shadow.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You see yourself as the observer right now, preferring to keep your lantern lit and your steps quiet while you navigate your own mind.

Reversed

You are acting like a wary stray person, suspicious of helpful hands and preferring to keep your secrets tucked away.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The world around you feels noisy or demanding, making your personal sanctuary feel both necessary and difficult to maintain.

Reversed

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You long for total enlightenment and self-sufficiency, yet you secretly fear that going too deep will leave you disconnected from others forever.

Reversed

You long for profound spiritual clarity but fear that, upon looking inside, you might find nothing at all.

X. Outcome

Upright

By honoring this inner journey, you will emerge with a renewed sense of purpose and a wisdom that functions as an unshakable compass.

Reversed

If you continue to avoid your truth, the path forward will remain obscured by unnecessary confusion and repeated mistakes.
