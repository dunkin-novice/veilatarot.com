Major Arcana · XV

The Devil

The Shadow

The Devil — Upright

attachment, addiction, obsession, bondage, shadow self

You feel trapped, but look closely: the chains are actually loose. You aren't stuck because you have to be; you're stuck because you haven't decided to walk away yet.

The Devil — Reversed

Release, Breaking free, Detachment, Awakening, Recovery

The cage is open, but your legs are still shaky. This is the detox phase—it feels raw and uncomfortable to be free, but don't you dare go back to that toxicity.

In the Celtic Cross spread

How The Devil reads in each of the ten Celtic Cross positions.

I. The Present

Upright

You are currently entwined in a situation defined by heavy attachment or a persistent habit. There is a sense of being 'stuck' in a pattern that offers immediate gratification but limits your true movement.

Reversed

You are at a pivotal turning point where the chains are loosening, and you're finally recognizing the toxic patterns or habits that have kept you small.

II. The Challenge

Upright

The friction lies in your own reluctance to let go of the 'golden chains.' You might be finding a strange comfort in your struggles or using a specific obsession to avoid facing a deeper, more uncomfortable truth.

Reversed

The friction lies in the 'comfort' of the cage; you might find yourself gravitating back toward familiar drama or old dependencies simply because freedom feels intimidating.

III. The Past

Upright

A recent period of indulgence or intense focus on physical needs has set the stage for this. You may have traded a bit of your freedom for a promise of security or pleasure that is now starting to feel heavy.

Reversed

You’ve recently survived a period of intense restriction or obsession that forced you to look at your darkest reflections in the mirror.

IV. The Future

Upright

The path ahead involves a direct confrontation with your own shadow. You will soon have to decide if the temporary highs are worth the long-term cost to your spirit.

Reversed

The next step involves a significant 'break'—an event or realization that confirms you can no longer be controlled by what used to haunt you.

V. Above · Conscious

Upright

Consciously, you are focused on material success, power, or satisfying a specific craving. You are looking for a way to master your surroundings through sheer force of will or external control.

Reversed

Consciously, you are focused on detoxing your life, setting boundaries, and reclaiming the personal power you feel you've signed away.

VI. Below · Unconscious

Upright

At the root, there is a deep-seated fear of powerlessness or lack. This hidden anxiety is driving you to overcompensate by clinging tightly to things, people, or behaviors that make you feel secure.

Reversed

Deep down, there is a lingering fear that you aren't strong enough to stay free, or an old belief that you don't deserve a life without struggle.

VII. Advice · Self

Upright

You may be seeing yourself as a victim of circumstance or biology, feeling as though you have no choice but to follow your current impulses. There’s a mix of defiance and self-sabotage in your current stance.

Reversed

You are presenting as the survivor who is still a bit shaky but determined to walk toward the light, refusing to be a victim of your own impulses anymore.

VIII. External Influences

Upright

The atmosphere around you feels restrictive or overly focused on appearances and material gain. Others may be encouraging your least healthy instincts or keeping you tied to a toxic dynamic.

Reversed

You are surrounded by reminders of your past self or people who may still benefit from you staying trapped, making your resolve even more crucial.

IX. Hopes & Fears

Upright

You long for the intensity of your current desires but are simultaneously terrified that you've lost the key to your own handcuffs. You fear you are becoming defined by what you crave.

Reversed

You long for total liberation and a fresh start, yet you fear the void that remains once the familiar noise of your 'demons' goes quiet.

X. Outcome

Upright

If this continues, the situation will lead to a realization that the chains were never locked. The ultimate destination is a choice: remain in the comfortable dark or acknowledge your power to walk away.

Reversed

If you continue this release, you will move into a phase of true self-governance, where your choices are driven by clarity rather than compulsion.
