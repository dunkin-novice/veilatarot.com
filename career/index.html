Career Tarot Summary / บทสรุปไพ่การงาน

Veila's Career tarot is a quiet, bilingual 3-card reading for the turning points of work — whether to take a job or leave it, whether you're valued or underpaid, what's blocking your growth, and what your real calling is. Money is read as direction — rising, tightening, opening — never as amounts or dates. Twenty-three questions, each drawn from the 78 Rider–Waite–Smith cards, for reflection, not prediction.
