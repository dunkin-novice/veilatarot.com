Should I go freelance?

“Should I go freelance?” carries weight on both sides — staying and leaving each cost something. This reading reads what you would be setting down, what you would be moving toward, and whether the timing is actually yours.

A three-card spread for this question

When you open this reading, the three cards are written for “Should I go freelance?” directly — not a generic layout. They read:

  1. Where You Stand Now
  2. What the Leap Holds
  3. The Money and the Move

How to read it

Read the three as both shores of the crossing: the first what you would leave, the second what you would move toward, the third the timing. The cards do not push you across — they let you weigh the leap with your eyes open.

Use this as a way to reflect and read direction, not a verdict to hand your life to. The final decision is always yours.
