Is this career right for me?

“Is this career right for me?” is a question about alignment, not just performance. The reading reflects what this path is asking of you, what it gives back, and whether the two are honestly meeting.

A three-card spread for this question

When you open this reading, the three cards are written for “Is this career right for me?” directly — not a generic layout. They read:

  1. Why This Path Called You
  2. Where It Doesn't Fit You
  3. Whether This Path Is Truly Yours

How to read it

Read the three as a mirror of alignment: the first reflects what the path asks, the second what it returns, the third where you stand between them. The cards do not grade you — they help you see whether this is a fit you are forcing or one that is already there.

Use this as a way to reflect and read direction, not a verdict to hand your life to. The final decision is always yours.
