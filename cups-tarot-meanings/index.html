The Suit of Cups

Cups are water — the element of feeling, intimacy, intuition, and what flows between people. When a Cup card lands, the question is usually emotional: who am I close to, what am I holding, what am I letting go of?

Cups don't argue. They ask. They reveal the temperature of a relationship, the unspoken thing under a conversation, the longing or the disappointment that's coloring how a situation actually feels. Even the harshest Cup cards (Five of Cups, Three of Swords' cousin in feeling) are about grief, not punishment.
